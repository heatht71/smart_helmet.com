    *Smart Helmet for Emergency Detection*

    Our innovative Smart Helmet is designed to enhance safety by detecting accidents and alerting emergency services. 
    Equipped with advanced sensors, including an accelerometer and GPS, this helmet automatically detects sudden 
    impacts and sends real-time location data via SMS or HTTP requests. In the event of an accident, the helmet 
    activates visual and audible alerts, and if not manually canceled within 30 seconds, it sends emergency 
    notifications to designated contacts and services. This cutting-edge technology aims to provide immediate 
    assistance and ensure timely intervention, offering peace of mind for riders and their families.
